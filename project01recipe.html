Brownies

A delicious treat for anytime of the day

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Vegetable Oil

  • 1 Cup Sugar

  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla

  • 2 Large Eggs

  • 1/4 Teaspoon of Baking Powder

  • 1/3 Cup cocoa powder

  • 1/4 Teaspoon of Salt

  • 1/2 Cup of Flour

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 365 degrees fahrenheit or 180 degrees celsius

  2. Mix oil and sugar until blended

  3. Mix all dry ingredients in a separate bowl

  4. Stir dry ingredients into the oil/ sugar mixture

  5. Pour into greases 9 x 9 square pan

  6. Bake for 2o minutes or until sides just start to pull away from the pan

  7. Cool completely before cutting
